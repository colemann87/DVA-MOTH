Rules for photos

The quality of your photo

Your photo must be:

  • clear, in focus and free from direct sunlight
  • no more than 4MB

What your photo must show

Your photo must:

  • show the registration number of the vehicle clearly
  • show the full front or back of the vehicle
  • not include any other vehicles

Enter registration number manually

You should enter the registration number and vehicle identification number (VIN) manually if:

  • the registration plate is over 2 lines
  • the vehicle has an old style registration plate
